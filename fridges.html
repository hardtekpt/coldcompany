Remote temperature sensor

With our state of the art accurate temperaturesensor we give our clients versatility when it comes to moniter their fridges. Using any mobile device, laptop or desktop users can login into their account through our website and monitor their fridge. The dashboard panel gives information on the sensor's last reading and the time the reading was taken. A graph is also shown with the temperatures from the last 24 hours. Moreover users can also view a table with the last 24 hours of readings. Finally they can set limits for temperature and be notified when those limits are crossed!
